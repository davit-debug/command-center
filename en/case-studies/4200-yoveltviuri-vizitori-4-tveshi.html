4200+ Monthly Visitors in 4 Months

Competition in the digital space for the healthcare sector is growing daily, especially as users primarily search on Google for health-related information. In such an environment, simply having a website is not enough; a proper SEO strategy is needed to make the site not only visible but also trustworthy.

SEO Started Before the Website Launch

10xSEO got involved in the project while the website was still in development—about a month and a half before launch. This window of time proved crucial: it was during this period that a solid foundation for technical SEO was prepared, the site structure was planned, and the content strategy was formulated.

In the initial phase, we worked not only on technical details, such as optimizing JavaScript-based pages, but also on strategic directions. We identified primary keywords, analyzed their relevance to specific pages in detail, and created a topical authority map—a structure that simultaneously answered user intent and met the requirements of search engines.

"We started working with 10xSEO before the site launch. It was the best decision we made."

— Project Manager

Strategic Use of an Aged Domain

One of the key strategic decisions was to use an old, authoritative domain. The domain already had a history, a certain number of backlinks, and signs of trustworthiness in the eyes of search engines.

10xSEO assessed the domain's current state, analyzed its past content, and planned how to integrate it into the new structure without losing existing authority. A redirect map was established to correctly forward traffic from old URLs to new pages while preserving SEO value.

Content Strategy Based on Search Intent

From the very beginning, the content strategy was planned based on search intent. Each article and blog post was created not just to provide general information, but to precisely answer the questions users have.

A crucial role in this process was played by 10xSEO's professional writer, who created content not only with in-depth knowledge of the medical field but also with the structural approach required for SEO.

The Result: 4200+ Visitors

Immediately after the site launch, we began monitoring rankings and strengthening pages that showed a quick response in search engines. Content updates, internal link optimization, and the addition of new topics were carried out incrementally.

Importantly, this growth trend is consistent—data from the last 28 days shows a 20-25% increase in visitors, which once again confirms that the results achieved were not a one-time success. 4,200+ monthly visitors in 4 months is not just a number—it is the result of a systematic SEO approach and strategic planning.
