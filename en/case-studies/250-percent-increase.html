250% Traffic Growth in 3 Months

We want to share a case study from 10xSEO where we increased organic traffic 2.5x in a short period, showcasing a typical yet powerful example of our process.

Strategy

We began the project with our standard strategy: we conducted keyword research and consulted with the business representatives to confirm their relevance and prioritize them.

We selected keywords that were already in the Top 10 but not in the first position, seeing the potential for rapid growth, and began our work.

In the initial stage, 8 pages were selected — relevant keywords were distributed across these pages (Keyword Mapping), their on-page SEO was optimized, and content was enhanced.

Several blog posts on relevant topics were added, and internal links were implemented. Website navigation was improved, and these pages were given higher priority.

After completing the internal work, we started link building — we strengthened the homepage with branded anchors and also built links to the selected pages.

"We knew the potential was there, but we didn't understand why we weren't growing. 10xSEO showed us exactly where the issue was."

— Marketing Manager

Result: 250% Growth

Traffic has increased by 250%, despite search trends generally declining sharply in August.
