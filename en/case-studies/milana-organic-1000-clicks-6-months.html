How We Grew Organic Traffic From Zero to 1,000+ Clicks in 6 Months

Milana, a premium dry cleaning service in Tbilisi, had zero digital visibility. In just 6 months, the 10xSEO team grew their organic traffic from nothing to over 1,000 monthly clicks by optimizing their Google Business Profile and implementing a cutting-edge AEO strategy.

The Starting Point: Zero Visibility

In Tbilisi, the name 'Milana' is synonymous with premium dry cleaning. The company had a newly built website, which was a perfect platform for future growth. However, at that stage, the site wasn't yet integrated with search engines, resulting in organic traffic being at an absolute zero.

The main goal of our partnership was to make Milana's high-quality service just as visible in Google search results. The business was running smoothly offline but needed the right digital positioning to align its online reputation with its real-world scale and quality.

Digital Visibility Indicators: Google Search Console Data

The most accurate measure of our effectiveness with search engines is the data from Google Search Console. The chart shown, covering the period from September 2025 to March 2026, clearly illustrates the site's visibility dynamics. While clicks and impressions were at zero initially, growth became steady and consistent after implementing our SEO strategy.

This data confirms that search algorithms recognized Milana's website as a relevant and trustworthy source. By the end of the 6-month period, organic traffic surpassed 1,000 clicks per month. The curve on the graph indicates that every technical change and piece of new content directly impacted user activity.

What Changed? The Digital Reality Before and After SEO

To grasp the project's scale, it's essential to analyze the starting point of Milana's digital journey. At the beginning of our collaboration, the website was practically invisible in organic search. Despite the business operating successfully offline, its digital visibility was minimal, and its Google Business Profile pages were not yet optimized for local competition.

After 6 months of work, this picture changed fundamentally. Where organic traffic was once zero, the site now generates over 1,000 targeted clicks monthly. The local search results are particularly impressive: interactions through Google Business Profile increased to 1,124. A significant breakthrough also occurred with Google rankings. Before our partnership, the site didn't appear in search results at all; now, Milana consistently ranks in the top 5 for the most competitive keywords.

"We had a website, but we were invisible on Google. Customers only came to us through recommendations."

— The Milana Team

The Strategy: The Path from Zero to Top Rankings

To make Milana's true potential understandable to Google's algorithms, we worked on several parallel tracks. The process began with an in-depth technical audit, because even the highest quality content is meaningless if a site is technically flawed for search engines. We meticulously analyzed the website's structure, corrected meta-data, and maximized page load speeds.

We placed a special emphasis on Local SEO, which is critically important for the dry cleaning industry. Our work on the Google Business Profile went beyond just updating basic data; we fundamentally transformed the digital positioning of both branches. By using high-quality visual content and implementing a new standard for managing customer feedback, we made each location as active and appealing as possible.

Simultaneously, we took the site's content to a whole new level. Our goal was to turn Milana's website into a trusted, expert platform. To do this, we developed each service area into its own authoritative page. Our team then leveraged this expert content base to implement Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) standards. This allowed Milana to appear organically in the answers that users now get directly from AI platforms.

The Results: Numbers That Prove the Strategy's Effectiveness

The strategic changes and our AI-focused approach exceeded expectations. After 6 months of work, Milana's digital metrics didn't just improve—they transformed qualitatively. When we talk about 1,000+ organic clicks, we're talking about over 1,000 potential customers who found the brand at their exact moment of need.

The impact of Local SEO was particularly impressive. Optimizing the Google Business Profile created a direct link between digital searches and the physical branches. Today, the number of monthly interactions (requests for directions, phone calls, checking business hours) reaches 1,124. This means that within a user's geographic area, Milana is the #1 choice.

Ultimately, our primary measure of success is real business results. The growth in site traffic and local visibility directly impacted sales channels. We now track over 100 direct calls and leads every month, confirming that the audience attracted through SEO is highly qualified and has clear intent. Milana is no longer solely dependent on existing recommendations; today, the brand actively creates new opportunities right where customers make their decisions.
